# Dropbox for Business Guided Tour

An immersive 3D tour experience showcasing Dropbox for Business features.

## Project Overview

The Dropbox for Business Guided Tour provides an interactive, 3D-enhanced exploration of Dropbox's business features. Using React, the application creates engaging visualizations and demonstrations that help users understand the platform's capabilities.
